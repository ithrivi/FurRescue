What We Do?

To assist homeless dogs and provide them a happy home, we aim to create a dog adoption website. Our website also offers a gathering area for those who appreciate spending time with dogs and are dog friendly.

The website is dedicated to disseminating knowledge about the dogs that are turned in to animal shelters and assisting in their adoption. Our main goal is to house all the canine necessities under one roof.

Why We Do?

More dogs are getting abandoned day by day as the owners tend to leave them at public places. Also, as a result of this the number of crimes against street dogs have also reached its peak.

Understanding the need for protecting them, we have arrived at the idea of creating a user-friendly dog adoption website particularly for the street dogs in order to protect and help them reach into safe hands.
